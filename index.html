Menu

Our restaurant menu features appetizers like soup, salad and more. You'll also find pasta, steak, and so much more on our menu! Whether you're looking for something sweet after dinner or just need a little snack between courses, we've got what you need. Here are our new additions to the menu:

Avocado toast

The avocado toast is an easy-to-make snack that can be enjoyed by anyone. It is made from a combination of avocados, bread, and mayonnaise. It is a healthy choice for people looking for a quick meal on the go or as an after-school snack.

Special offer: only 3$

BBQ ground beef

The BBQ ground beef dish looks like a piece of meat with barbecue sauce, but it isn't really a piece of meat. It has a texture that's soft and squishy, and it has a taste that's savory and salty.
